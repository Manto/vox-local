# Privacy Policy for VoxLocal

**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Privacy Commitment

VoxLocal is a Chrome extension that provides text-to-speech functionality. We respect your privacy and collect **no data** from users.

## No Data Collection

- We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- No analytics, tracking, or telemetry data is gathered
- No cookies are used
- No data is sent to external servers

## Everything Runs in Your Browser

- All text-to-speech processing happens locally in your browser
- Audio generation uses a model stored on your device
- No internet connection required after initial setup
- Your selected text never leaves your device

## Local Storage

VoxLocal stores user preferences and model files locally on your device. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Can be cleared by removing the extension

Questions? Open an issue at: https://github.com/Manto/vox-local/issues
